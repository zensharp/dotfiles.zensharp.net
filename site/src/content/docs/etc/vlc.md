## Windows
1. Add `%programfiles%\VideoLAN\VLC` to `PATH`.

## WSL
1. Add `%programfiles%\VideoLAN\VLC` to `PATH`.
1. Create a shim at `%programfiles%/VideoLAN/VLC/vlc`.
